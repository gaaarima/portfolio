About Me

Biography

I am a UX designer who believes the best digital experiences are built by blending empathy with innovative technology. I focus on creating integrated, AI-powered solutions that simplify complex workflows and keep people motivated.

My recent work includes designing inventory and fitness products that turn scattered tasks into clear, guided flows — helping users stay organized and confident in their daily decisions.

My process begins with deep user research: listening to real pain points, mapping journeys, and synthesizing insights into design decisions that are both human-centered and technically feasible. I care about accessibility, clarity, and creating moments of delight within functional systems.
